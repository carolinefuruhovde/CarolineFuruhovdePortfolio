is an app inspired by the 10-50-10 study system.

SKETCHES

The idea around Pust Ut, is to study in intervals inspired by the 50-50-30 method from “Topp Karakter Systemet”. The greatest enemy for any student during their study break is not using their breaks to properly relax to gain energy for their next studysession. The app provides information and tips on how to utilize your study breaks so you can be at your most efficient self when you study. This includes meditation, sleep, eating and other activities that are proven to help healthy learning and consentration.

The sketches are a representation og the actovities I found during my research, that where the most energy saving and work effctive activities you could do while partiocipating in a lot of head-grinding. Things such as drinking enough liquids, sleeping, exercise, meditation etc. The illustrations are meant to be motivating, and gives an aesthetically fresh look to the timer.
